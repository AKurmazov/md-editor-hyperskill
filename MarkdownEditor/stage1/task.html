Description

Welcome to the first stage of the project! 

If you ever worked with markdown, you will probably recognize the syntax here:

	# The Beatles
One may say that they were the most famous **rock band** ever.
The legendary **line-up** comprised the following people:
* *John Lennon*
* *Paul McCartney*
* *George Harrison*
* *Ringo Starr*

Yes! It is a raw markdown code, and it is to be translated to something looking like this:

The Beatles

One may say that they were the most famous rock band ever.
The legendary line-up comprised the following people:

  • John Lennon
  • Paul McCartney
  • George Harrison
  • Ringo Starr

 

Objectives

In this stage, I propose you to do exactly the opposite you've been shown In the description part: you will be given a fancy-looking markdown snippet, and you will have to determine its source code. By all means, you will need to get acquainted with the basics of the syntax. I suggest using this tutorial — it is easy to follow and covers all the necessary topics.

Here's the snippet of markdown to analyze:

John Lennon

or John Winston Ono Lennon, was one of The Beatles.
Here are the songs he wrote I like the most:

  • Imagine
  • Norwegian Wood
  • Come Together
  • In My Life
  • Hey Jude (that was McCartney)

 

Examples

Let's assume that we gave you this snippet to translate:

The List of Albums

During their history, a lot of great albums were released.
These are my favorite:

  1. Abbey Road
  2. Rubber Soul
  3. Revolver
  4. A Hard Day's Night

 

Then, we would expect you to print to the console  the following code:

	# The List of Albums
During their history, a lot of great albums were released.
These are my favorite:
1. Abbey Road
2. Rubber Soul
3. Revolver
4. A Hard Day's Night
